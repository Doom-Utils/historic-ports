DOSDOOM CREDITS

Due to unique the way DOSDoom has been developed is has been necessary to have a page dedicated to the people who have contributed in someway to DOSDoom.

For the latest info, visit the DOSDoom Website at http://www.frag.com/dosdoom

The people who have helped shape DOSDoom

Id Software:
-They wrote a great game called doom and they were kind enough to release the source, I could go on, but I wouldn't have the time to include everything.

Chi Hoang:
-This is the person who ported the linux source code released by id software to a DOS Executable (hence the name: 'DOSDoom'), he carried the project until Version 0.61, its worth a note that all the ports written for DOS so far have directly or indirectly used a version of DOSDoom as their base. We wouldn't be here now without his effort - he deserves a big "thank you".

DOSDoom Team:
-The team currently responsible for the up keep and continuing developement of DOSDoom, its worth a note that all the people listed here have all spent a lot of time and effort before the team was ever formed prior to Version 0.61 and their names would no doubt feature numerous times in the contributing authors section.
